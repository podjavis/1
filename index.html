He returns to the city — a dark, cold, empty neon city where night blurs the streets and reality feels simulated. Wrapped in a long coat, he moves through the night with the calm determination of a man on a mission. Every corner echoes with old memories, old mistakes, old enemies.

FULL M00N — The Prologue

You’ve been here before.
It always starts the same way.

You begin with nothing — weightless, almost invisible, just trying to exist.
Then, you rise. You climb. You feel the warmth of being up, the rush of becoming something more.
You taste life at its peak — alive, unstoppable, untouchable.
And then… it slips. Slowly, then all at once.
The high fades. The lights dim. You fall down.

You try to hold on, but the noise inside grows silent.
Something dies — maybe the dream, maybe the part of you that believed.

And there you are again… nothing.
The circle closes, only to start again.

You lived this cycle so many times that it became you — comforting, toxic, addictive.
Until the night you realized the moon was full, and it was time to break free.
